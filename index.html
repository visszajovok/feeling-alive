May 17 – Race Day

My Story

Hello, my name is Míra.

On May 17th, exactly one day after my 18th birthday, I will race a Half Ironman.

Six years ago pain entered my life. In 2024 I was diagnosed with chronic pain.

Instead of stepping back, I decided to train for something that once felt impossible.

This race is about showing that pain may be part of life, but it does not define what we are capable of.
